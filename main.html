About the Congress

Get to know more about PowerNex

Tunisia faces a key moment for growth through green technologies.
PowerNex aims to protect resources, promote innovation, and build sustainable industries that support both communities and the environment.
Don’t miss the opportunity—join us on April 12!

Learn more

Technical challenge

Smart Microgrid for Remote Communities with Autonomous Maintenance Robots

Develop smart microgrids powered by renewable energy for remote communities.
Integrate autonomous robots for maintenance and Axe Medical for healthcare support.
Focus on scalable, sustainable energy and medical solutions for underserved areas.

Learn more

Contest

Digital Twins' Impact in Industry

Explore the role of digital twins in the electronics industry.
Research applications like smart manufacturing, predictive maintenance, and supply chain optimization.
Encourage innovation and prepare students for future industrial advancements.
